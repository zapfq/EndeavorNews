You can't deny that no one can say that travelling brings a bad mood.

Although some people think that travel is "going from where you are used to to where others are used to", it is indeed the happiest thing in the world. It's like a "time travel machine" that briefly takes us out of our unsurprising lives and into the lives of others. It can also relieve the pressure we have been carrying on the body, throw off all the shackles of the body, to see the vast world.

There is a wealth of scientific research that proves that exploring a new place can help improve mental and psychological health. Travelling can keep people in a better mood. We all choose to travel to a country that we are interested in, and the scenery along the way will make us happy and not easily fall into negative and sad emotions.

Besides, travelling can broaden our eyes and make it easier to accept the culture and customs from all over the world. The world is vast, and cultures and ideas are changeable. Being in the same place for long periods of time can narrow our vision, narrow our minds, and make us more likely to criticise the actions of others for our inability to accept differences. But travel allows us to see and hear more different voices from other countries. It was a very valuable experience.

A travel aficionado and founder of The Passport Memorandum, Marta Estevez said: “I think people, in general, are not meant to be tied down to just one place their entire lives. My life feels most fulfilling when I’m outside, living through new experiences and learning,”

Besides, according to a study of Cornell University, the anticipation of a trip can increase our happiness substantially, even more than the anticipation of acquiring something tangible, for example, a new car.

Last but not least, travel can improve our ability to solve problems. Everything on the journey is unknown and unpredictable. Therefore, there will always be unavoidable contingencies. When you have only one person, we find ways to solve problems, which increases our ability in it. In this process, feeling in control gives us confidence and makes us more independent.

Travel is the easiest way to gain happiness. Therefore, in the feeling of ordinary life, or the mood is not good, might as well take a trip, explore those who have not seen the scenery, to find those who have been ignored by the surprise feeling.
