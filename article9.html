How Are Youths Rectifying The Pollution Problems.

Environment News by Fu Qi.

The youth of Malaysia are putting in all their efforts to fix our environment and create a better place for us to live.

KUALA LUMPUR: The youth of Malaysia are putting in all their efforts to fix our environment and create a better place for us to live.

An example of this would be UN Environment recruiting scouts and girl guides in various countries to participate in bidding on plastic pollution and marine litter. (The Star, 2021)

Even secondary school students were involved and moved the community to reduce plastic usage in the Plastic Tide Turners Challenge.

“From reducing single-use plastic to recycling more effectively, to finding sustainable alternatives to plastic, the solutions to plastic pollution are within our grasp, ” says one of the helpers.

The youths believe that through social media, they can educate the public about plastic pollution and promote recycling through organising activities in their local neighbourhood.

Another group of youth exemplify themselves as river rangers to protect their river within the territory of Johor to ensure its safety and pollution-free. (NST, 2022)

Persatuan Lestari Alam Malaysia president Rashdan Rashid stated the campaign is meant to protect essential resources.

The youth river rangers are there to serve and protect their rivers regularly to prevent damage and pollution, so they are a crucial part of this whole operation.

The allocated 4.5 million Ringgit by the government helps boost the process of cleansing the river of pollutants and would help continue to sustain the effort in doing so.

The added budget also meant for more knowledgeable experts in ecology and biodiversity to come in and support the rangers in their efforts.

Rashdan believed that community is a crucial part of the river ranger operation, especially with the added knowledge, revitalising the river would ensure that Johor's biome remains diverse and sustainable.
